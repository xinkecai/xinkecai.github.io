My Productive Browser Setup

A few small browser habits can save a lot of time during research sessions. These are the ones I use most often:

  • Pin frequently used tabs like email and calendar.
  • Use bookmark folders by topic rather than one giant list.
  • Keep one tab group for active tasks and close it at the end of the day.
  • Use a read-later extension for long articles.

Productivity comes from reducing friction. Keep your setup simple enough that it helps, not distracts.
